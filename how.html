HOW IT WORKS?

Anyone can join UNMASK, Free and anonymously as the website never request any kind of personal information for registry. UNMASK will never collect or share personal information from its users. Once users had sign up they can freely start to create profiles or posting about other persons they wish to "unmask." Members can use full, legal names for these profiles.

In order to prevent false accusations, false blaming or bullying, we have improved 2 systems on our website: 1. To be able to create a profile or post something is an important requirement that users also upload any hard evidence – video, audio, photos, documents, links from other social networks they have of the person's wrongdoing. 2. Once a profile or post is live, the user community can vote on the accuracy of material, FALSE or TRUE. Enough "False" votes will result in an automatically profile or post deletion.

Once profiles or postings are created, there is no possibility to delete or modify it. Unmask offers a free environment for users to post or write anything about any person. We also have improved a SEARCH engine; so once our data base becomes with many profiles stored, any user will be able to search easily any person by his legal name.
